                                  OPENADS
                        PRIVACY AND DATA USAGE POLICY

                This Privacy and Data Usage Policy explains how
                        Openads Limited ("Openads")
                    collects and uses information via the
                Openads website and Openads adserver software.


    1. What is the Openads Community?
The Openads Community is made up of website publishers and advertisers
who use Openads software to create, promote, manage and deliver online
ad campaigns. To become a member of the Openads Community and take
advantage of the benefits that this brings, you need to register and
create a profile and download Openads software.


    2. What type of information does Openads collect?
Openads collects a variety of data listed below relating to members
of the Openads Community.

Profile Data: This is information that you provide when you register
and create a profile with Openads. It includes your username and
password, a contact email address and other information that you
provide during the set-up process.

Configuration Data: If you have enabled the "check for updates"
setting in the Openads software, then Openads will collect information
relating to the configuration of your server used to run Openads.
This information is used to identify which product update alerts
are relevant to you, and to profile the community so that we can
focus development and testing efforts on common adserver configurations.
This configuration data includes:
    * The version and basic configuration options of the adserver.
    * Type and version information about other software which Openads
      depends on, including: the webserver, PHP and MySQL.

Website Data: Openads also collects data regarding anonymous visitors
to the Openads website including: the IP address of the user's
computer (which may include a domain name), the referring URL (if any)
and details of the Internet browser software used by the user to view
the Openads website. If you use the online Contact us feature of the
website, we collect your name, email address and details of your message
to enable us to respond to your inquiry.


    3. Does Openads collect personally identifiable information?
Openads only collects and processes personally identifiable information
that you provide to us when registering as an Openads Community user,
using the online Contact us feature of the website or participating
in the forums.


    4. How does Openads use Profile Data?
Openads uses your Profile Data to manage your online profile and
to send you details of software updates. If you have indicated through
your Openads profile that you wish to receive such information, then
we may also send you information about new products and/or services
that Openads provides. You can opt-out of receiving future marketing
material by changing the relevant setting in your Openads profile or by
writing to us via our online contact form at
http://www.openads.org/contact-us/.


    5. Transfer of information to third parties
Your individual Configuration Data and Profile Data will not be shared
with or re-sold to anyone, except where we are required to do so by law
or if Openads is sold or sells a portion of its assets which includes
user information.


    6. Cookies
We use cookies to help you use the Openads website and adserver
software but do not use cookies to capture personally identifiable
information.  We use these cookies to identify you as a valid Openads
user, to ensure that no one else can simultaneously log in and use
your Openads profile and to help personalise your Openads experience in
conjunction with your Openads profile.

For more information about how cookies work and how to disable them,
go to http://www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/. However, if you
choose to disable cookies, you won't be able to enjoy a significant
portion of the Openads website and software functionality.


    7. Questions/feedback
If you have any questions or feedback regarding Openads' data collection
or usage practices, you can contact us online at hello@openads.org.


    8. Changes to this Privacy and Data Usage Policy
We may change this Privacy and Data Usage Policy from time to time.
These changes will be brought to your attention by publishing them
on the Openads website (http://www.openads.org).
